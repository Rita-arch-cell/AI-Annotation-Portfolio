# LEGAL REASONING EVALUATION: McGUIRE v. NEWARK
## Employment Discrimination Analysis - Prompt & Rubric

---

## THE PROMPT

You are a legal analyst reviewing a potential employment discrimination case. Based on the four sources provided, analyze whether Kayla McGuire has viable claims for:

1. **Gender-based discrimination** in violation of Ohio R.C. 4112.02(A)
2. **Hostile work environment sexual harassment** under Ohio law

### Sources Provided:
- **Source 1:** `Ohio_RC_4112_02.pdf` - Ohio statutory text prohibiting employment discrimination
- **Source 2:** `Hampel_Framework.pdf` - Ohio Supreme Court hostile work environment legal standard
- **Source 3:** `McDonnell_Douglas_Framework.pdf` - Employment discrimination analysis framework
- **Source 4:** `McGuire_Case_Facts.pdf` - Complete case facts, timeline, and evidence

### Your Analysis Must:
1. Apply the **McDonnell Douglas three-step framework** to determine if McGuire establishes a prima facie case of gender discrimination
2. Apply the **Hampel four-part test** to evaluate her hostile work environment claim
3. Compare McGuire's treatment to the similarly-situated male trainee (Derrick Beach) using evaluation data
4. Evaluate whether the harassment was sufficiently **"severe or pervasive"** under Ohio law using the totality of circumstances
5. Assess the employer's response to McGuire's complaints

### Required Structure:
Format your response as a legal memorandum with clear sections for:
- **Part I:** Gender Discrimination Analysis (McDonnell Douglas)
- **Part II:** Hostile Work Environment Analysis (Hampel)
- **Part III:** Conclusion and Strength of Claims
